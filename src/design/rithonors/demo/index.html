Welcome to the recently-redesigned home page for the Honors Program at RIT. From here you can find information on the Honors Program in general and the Honors Council and Committees. You may want to take a look at The Greening of RIT, which is the website for the Greening of RIT Environmental Science Honors class. It hosts the results of a survey designed by the class to determine how the student body perceives the green-spaces around RIT.

Also, there is a forum where Honors students can discuss ideas relating to the Committees, the Council, the Honors Program as a whole, or any general topics.
