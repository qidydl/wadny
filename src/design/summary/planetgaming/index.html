Welcome - April 8, 1999 :: 9:04pm Welcome to planetgaming!
Welcome to the all-new planetgaming! This site will be your number-one source for gaming news and information. As part of the FPSID expansion project, planetgaming was created to provide a home for other gaming genres. All of FPSID's content will be transferred over to pg as soon as possible. We have a team of news and information gathering people, and they'll collect, paraphrase, compress, and format all the stuff they find into our database of gaming stuff. We'll also host other sites, though of course they won't be information-based sites. We mainly want sites that will provide addons, like levels and TC's and models and such; as well as services, like designing all these levels and TC's and models. We'll also host clans, but the other sites are given priority. So go forth, and bask in our SSI-enabled database. :-)
